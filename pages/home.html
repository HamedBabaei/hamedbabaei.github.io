I'm Hamed, a researcher at TIB and currently, I'm involved in the Neural-Symbolic SCholarly InnovatioN EXTraction (SciNEXT) project in collaboration with Open Research Knowledge Graph (ORKG) project at TIB, with aim of employing ML and NLP techniques to scholarly text.

I earned my bachelor’s degree in computer science and my master’s degree in Soft Computing and AI. I worked as an NLP Researcher for different companies for almost three years befor joining TIB. I have experience in both academics and industry. Back in the time that I wasn't in TIB, in different companies, I have done research, analysis, designing, and implementation of various models in the field. In academia, I kept my research on different topics, ranging from Social Media tasks to Language Models and Knowledge Graphs. I have worked in a team of data scientists as a team leader, NLP specialist, and researcher.


My research interests comprise the topics in area of Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Knowledge Graphs, Large Language Models, Ontology Learning, and Question Answering.
